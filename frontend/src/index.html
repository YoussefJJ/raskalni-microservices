What is Raskalni?

The industry’s main activity is to take the plastic from the “barbecha”, recycle it then sell them with a lower price to the companies that will consume the plastic.

History of Raskalni

Raskalni is a company founded in 2004 by the ambitious ex-googler Zeineb Haj Hamdi to build a network of Garbage Collectors "Barbecha" to combat the excess of plastic trash by collecting litter and selling it to companies to recycle it.

Missions and ambitions

Number #1 priority is to have healthy, clean environment for next generations

To hire more than 40 000 scavengers by 2025

To drop the percentage of soil pollution to 43% by 2025
